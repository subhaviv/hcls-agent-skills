# Appeal Letter Template

Populate every `[bracketed]` field from the input. If a value isn't provided, leave the bracket and add it to the reviewer checklist.

```
[Provider / Facility Letterhead]
[Provider Name] · [NPI / Tax ID]
[Address] · [Phone] · [Fax]

[Date]

[Payer Name]
Attn: Appeals / Grievance Department
[Payer Appeals Address]

RE: Appeal of Claim Denial — Request for Reconsideration

Patient Name:        [Patient Name]
Member ID:           [Member ID]
Claim Number:        [Claim Number]
Date(s) of Service:  [DOS]
Service / Procedure: [CPT/HCPCS + description]
Diagnosis:           [ICD-10 + description]
Denial Reason Code:  [CARC/RARC + description]

Dear Appeals Reviewer,

We appeal the denial of the above claim, denied on [denial date] for:
"[quoted denial rationale]." We request the denial be overturned and
the claim paid for the reasons below.

CLINICAL SUMMARY
[Concise clinical narrative tying diagnosis to the service ordered.]

BASIS FOR APPEAL
[Argument mapped to the specific denial reason — show why the denial
is incorrect and how the service meets the payer's coverage criteria.]

SUPPORTING POLICY / GUIDELINE
[Cite only real, verifiable policy/guideline — never fabricate.]

REQUESTED ACTION
Overturn the denial and process claim [Claim Number] for payment.

ENCLOSURES
[List each attached document.]

Sincerely,
[Name, Credentials]
[Title] · [Department]
[Phone] · [Email]
```

## Formatting notes
- Keep the clinical summary tight and factual — reviewers skim.
- Quote the payer's denial language verbatim so the appeal maps directly to it.
- One denial reason per letter; if multiple, address each under BASIS FOR APPEAL.
